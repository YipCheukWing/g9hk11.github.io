Conclusion

Healthy  changes everything.

In conclusion, our redesigned menu is healthier than the original one. Our report proves that healthy food can also taste good without using harmful seasoning, and lunch menu could be much more healthy. But, even with healthy lunch, student still need to exercise more and eat less in other meals.
