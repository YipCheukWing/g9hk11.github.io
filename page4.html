Redesigning

Better in health, better in taste.

  1. Observing - we found out that fat and sugar is the main ingredient that made school lunch unhealthy, 2 out of 3 choice on the lunch menu have excessive amount of fat than what the department of health suggested, the worst being 33.9g more than the standard.
  2. Removing ingrediants - To completely improve healthiness of the lunches, some ingredient need to be changed completely like having one less egg in the egg croissant to lower the amount of fat
  3. Subsitution - Fat will be handy to deal with because meat like beef and pork contains a lot of fat, we used chicken as a substitutional meat because chicken fillet doesn’t contain a lot of fat. And salad dressing also contains a lot of unhealthy fat. As for sugar, we use bagel instead of croissant and use coconut sugar instead of honey to lower amount of sugar.
